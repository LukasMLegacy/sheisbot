Frequently Asked Questions

Earnings vary widely depending on follower count, engagement rate, subscription price, and content quality. Creators with 50K-100K followers typically earn $1,000-$5,000/month, while those with 100K-500K followers can earn $5,000-$20,000+/month. Top creators with 500K+ followers regularly earn $50,000+/month.
The main factors are: 1) Follower count and reach, 2) Engagement rate, 3) Subscription price, 4) Content quality and consistency, 5) Free trial conversion rate, 6) Niche attractiveness, 7) Message and tip engagement, 8) Retention rate.
Recommended subscription prices range from $3.99-$19.99/month depending on your niche and content exclusivity. Most successful creators charge $4.99-$9.99. Lower prices attract more subscribers but reduce per-subscriber revenue. The sweet spot for most creators is $6-$8/month.
Free trials significantly increase signups (typically 2-3x more) but conversion rates to paid subscriptions are lower. However, offering free trials usually results in higher overall revenue despite lower per-signup value, as the increased volume compensates for lower conversion.
Our estimates are based on aggregated industry benchmarks across thousands of creator profiles. The model accounts for engagement-based quality scoring, price elasticity of demand, and niche-specific conversion rates. Use this as a directional guide — actual earnings depend on content quality, audience intent, and your specific engagement with subscribers.
Beginners (under 50K followers) should aim for $100-$500/month in the first year. Intermediate creators (50K-200K followers) can target $1,000-$5,000/month. Advanced creators (200K+ followers) can realistically aim for $10,000+/month with consistent content and strong engagement.
