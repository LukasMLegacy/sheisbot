🔍 Discovery & Traffic

How the platform helps new fans find you.

Fanvue

Built-in search, explore page, and category browsing. Creators get organic traffic without spending on ads. Internal recommendation algorithm promotes active creators.

OnlyFans

No discovery features. Creators must drive 100% of traffic themselves through social media, ads, or link-in-bio tools.

Fansly

Basic explore page with limited browsing. Some internal traffic but significantly less than dedicated discovery platforms.

Patreon

Limited discovery via categories. Most creators rely entirely on external audiences from YouTube, podcasts, or social media.

Our Verdict

🏆 Best for New Creators: Fanvue

If you're starting out and don't have a massive following, Fanvue's built-in discovery engine gives you the best chance of getting found organically. The lower 15% fee means you keep more, and weekly payouts help with cash flow. AI creators have no better option.

📊 Best Brand Recognition: OnlyFans

OnlyFans has the strongest brand name, which means fans already know what it is. If you have a large existing following and can drive your own traffic, the brand recognition advantage can boost conversion rates.

🎨 Best for Non-Adult Creators: Patreon

For podcasters, educators, artists, and musicians, Patreon's tier system and community features are purpose-built. The lowest fees (5-12%) make it ideal for creators who don't need PPV or tipping.
